# Decision Review Example

Use the Multi-Agent Devil’s Advocate Review Skill.

## Review Packet

Review Mode: Decision Review

Target: Choosing whether to adopt a new AI coding tool across a team

Risk category: Productivity, security, maintainability, cost, governance

Relevant user flow or business process: Non-engineers and engineers may both use AI tools to prototype internal apps

Expected behavior: The team gains speed without creating unreviewed, insecure, or unmaintainable systems

Out of scope: Full vendor procurement process, enterprise legal negotiation

## Prompt

Review this decision using structured disagreement. Recommend whether we should approve, pilot, reject, or require more evidence.
